Peace of mind

Take a sit please,
Try to be at ease,
And embrace the breeze,
Or else life will not cease,

Throwing you at your knees,
Begging for what you seek,
Blindly ignoring what you need,
Failing to take heed,

You'll lose track of the basics,
Dismantle the schematics,
Become one of those fanatics,
Living lives that are dramatic.

Dance

A language that transcends near and far
With every graceful movement and leap
It touches our souls, so deep

In the sway of the body, a story is told
Of emotions, dreams and secrets untold
The rythm carries us, like waves on the shore
As we dance, we find ourselves thirsting for more

Beauty lying in its expression,
A form of art that knows no repression
It speaks through bodies, in every twist and turn,
Pop and wave
A universal language, we all yearn

From elegance to fierceness,
From rythm to flow,
The bliss i desire more,
Dance captivates, it sets our spirits free,
A celebration of movement, for all to see.

What better way can we express what we feel,
Than to bask in its beauty with a step
A step into a whole new world

Father's embrace

The peace I seek is found,
Found in nothing but,
Nothing but the arms,
The arms open wide,
The arms open wide to receive me

I'm talking about the arms that carry me,
That carry me to a place so ethereal,
In this place I find solace,
What a wonder to know that it is my father's arms,
That fully embrace me

Shielded, I'm cradled, comforted and secure,
He loved me very much,
That he became flesh,
Only to suffer for my sake,
And he died to pave the way,

That we may meet again,
In a place called paradise,
Where I will take a rest,
Knowing i'm safe,
Within my father's reach.
